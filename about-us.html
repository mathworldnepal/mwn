Creating an Intellectual Mathematics Platform in Nepal

About Us

We're a startup for Mathematics research, content creation, tutoring, and digital networking in Nepal. We intend to build an intellectual mathematics platform, where nepalese students, regardless of races, ethnic origins, sexual orientations, believes, disabilites, and financial hardships can find a strong foundation to prepare for national and international math competitions.

2021

Math World Nepal was founded in 2021

35+ Team Members

Across 5 provinces and 15 districts

1000+ Learners

We have grown to more than 1K students from all over Nepal

We stand in building free Math resources in Nepal

Because We stand in building free Math resources

We aim ...

Share digital content in form of lectures, blogs, articles, and animated videos

Create an open platform for discussion of research ideas and theories

Outreach under-previleged students and provide free tutoring services

Conduct data-analysis in societal problems and write a paper which proposes mathematical solution
